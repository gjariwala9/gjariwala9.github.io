About Me

A Bit About Me

Hey there! I'm Gaurav — a Senior Machine Learning Engineer who genuinely loves building things that think. Whether it's a multi-agent system that hunts for deals while I sleep, or an LSTM architecture that learns to play like a human, I get most excited when a complex idea finally clicks into a working system.

My journey started with a curiosity about how machines can learn patterns the way we do, and that curiosity hasn't slowed down. Over the past few years, I've had the chance to architect end-to-end ML pipelines at IGT, co-invent 16 U.S. patents, and dive deep into the world of LLMs, RAG, and agentic AI. I've published research at conferences like CANAI and IEEE INOCON, and I hold a Master's in Computer Science from Dalhousie University.

What drives me is the full picture — not just training a model, but owning the entire lifecycle from messy data to a deployed system that actually delivers value. I'm equally comfortable fine-tuning a model with QLoRA as I am designing the orchestration layer that ties seven agents together. If there's a hard problem at the intersection of AI and real-world impact, I want to be working on it.
